# Code of Conduct

BCP is a small technical project. Keep discussion sharp, respectful, and
evidence-based.

## Expected behavior

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Critique ideas with technical reasoning.
- Prefer reproducible evidence over assertion.
- Assume good intent, but do not hand-wave weak arguments.
- Keep feedback actionable.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks or harassment
- Doxxing or exposing private information
- Discriminatory language
- Repeated bad-faith disruption
- Spam, hype, or deliberately misleading claims

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, or pull requests
that damage the working environment of the project.

Serious or repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from
the repository.
